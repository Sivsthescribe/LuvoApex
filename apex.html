Thabiso's Story

The journey from Tembisa to purpose-driven leadership

Growing up in the Elindinga section of Tembisa, life was far from easy. But those challenges taught me the true meaning of hard work, perseverance, and resilience. My faith in God has always been my guiding light, and my family has been my steadfast anchor.

It was my older brother who first introduced me to football at the age of eight, and from that moment, it became more than just a game—it became my passion and my purpose. Through the highs and lows, I learnt that dreams are worth chasing relentlessly, no matter the obstacles.

"Now, I want to pay it forward. Through my foundation, I strive to show young people from similar backgrounds that their circumstances do not define their destiny."

With hard work, sacrifice, and discipline, anything is possible. I believe in empowering the next generation to rise, to dream boldly, and to reach their fullest potential.

The Thabiso Monyane Foundation is built on the principles that shaped my journey - resilience, faith, and the belief that every young person deserves the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their starting point.

About Us

Founded by Thabiso James Monyane

The Thabiso Monyane Foundation is a youth-driven, impact-focused organization committed to empowering South African youth through education, sports, and community development. Our mission is to create opportunities, build resilience, and inspire purpose in every young person we reach.

Vision

To nurture a generation of purpose-driven youth who lead with integrity, resilience, and hope.

Mission

To empower young people through education, sports, and social programs that develop leadership, self-worth, and community upliftment.

Our Programs

Initiatives that create lasting impact

Education and Sport

Empowering youth through access to quality education and sports mentorship programs. We provide school support, and sports training that foster discipline, teamwork, and academic success.

Thabiso Connect

A business hub initiative linking township organizations and entrepreneurs to mentorship, and market opportunities. It's a bridge between township innovation and mainstream enterprise.

School Nutrition

Fighting hunger in schools by providing nutritious meals and food packs to ensure that learners can focus, grow, and succeed.

Men Conversation

Safe spaces for men to engage, reflect, and redefine masculinity through open dialogue about mental health, responsibility, and positive role modeling.

Thabiso Sport Clinic

Community sports clinics offering coaching, sports injury rehabilitation, mentorship, and life skills development for young athletes — nurturing talent both on and off the field.
